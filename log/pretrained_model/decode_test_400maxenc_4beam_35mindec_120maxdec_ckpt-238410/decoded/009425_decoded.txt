2016 article originally posted in essf image from hoodwich native americans and their allies won an important battle in their fight to prevent the dakota access pipeline to go through historic tribal lands of the sioux , and under part of the missouri river where the sanding rock sioux reservation gets its drinking water .
the battle was joined by the u.s. army corps of engineers -lrb- ace -rrb- , when it issued an ultimatum that the protest at the site where the pipeline is scheduled to go under the river must be disbanded .
