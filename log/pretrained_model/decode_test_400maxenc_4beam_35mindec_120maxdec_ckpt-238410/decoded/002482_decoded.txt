by wendy koch , national geographic published january 20 , 2015 the scenic yellowstone river has suffered its second sizable oil spill in four years , prompting truckloads of drinking water to be shipped into the eastern montana city of glendive .
the latest spill is not expected to affect yellowstone national park , about 350 miles upstream .
some oil from the weekend spill got into a water supply along the river that serves about 6,000 people in glendive .
