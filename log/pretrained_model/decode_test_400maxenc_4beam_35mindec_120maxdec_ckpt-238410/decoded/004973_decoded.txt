the destructive and murderous train set in motion centuries ago continues to run , this time with judge boasberg as conductor and his oct. 11 ruling that the dakota access pipeline could continue to pump oil pending an environmental review by the army corps of engineers .
the standing rock sioux tribe sued the corps in july , 2016 contending that the pipeline destroyed sacred indigenous sites and threatens the water quality of the standing rock reservation .
the tribe has consistently filed motions to stop the flow of oil while litigation is ongoing .
