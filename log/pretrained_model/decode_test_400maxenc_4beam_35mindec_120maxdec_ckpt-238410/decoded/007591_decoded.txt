pipeline is an approximately 1,172 mile pipeline that would connect the bakken and three forks oil production areas in north dakota to an existing crude oil terminal near pakota , illinois .
the pipeline is 30 inches in diameter and is projected to transport approximately 470,000 barrels of oil per day , with a capacity as high as 570,000 barrels .
the current proposed pipeline route would cross lake oahe , an army corps of engineers project on the missouri river .
