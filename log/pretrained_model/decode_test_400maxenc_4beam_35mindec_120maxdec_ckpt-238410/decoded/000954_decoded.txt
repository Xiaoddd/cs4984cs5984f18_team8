sequoyah fuels corporation was opened by kerr-mcgee in 1970 to convert yellowcake uranium into uranium hexafluoride , a compound that produces fuel for nuclear reactors .
the company switched hands several times over the years before closing in 1993 after several releases of hazardous chemicals .
in january 1986 , one worker was killed and dozens more were injured after a cylinder of uranium hexafluoride ruptured .
