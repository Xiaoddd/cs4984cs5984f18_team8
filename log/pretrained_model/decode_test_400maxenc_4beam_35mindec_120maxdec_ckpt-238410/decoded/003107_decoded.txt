the headwaters are approximately 10 miles north of the headwaters of the north branch of the potomac river and pass through silver lake before flowing north-northeast into garrett county , maryland , then flowing north past oakland and roughly parallel to the west virginia border , separated by approximately 3 miles -lrb- 5 km -rrb- .
the river is impounded by the 184-foot-high -lrb- 56 m -rrb- youghiogheny dam to form the youghiogheny river lake .
