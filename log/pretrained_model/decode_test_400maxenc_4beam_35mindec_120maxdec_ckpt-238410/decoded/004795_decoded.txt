environmental and native american groups in minnesota are gearing up to thwart the replacement of an aging pipeline .
the minnesota pipeline at the center of this latest battle is a replacement for an existing pipeline called line 3 .
built in the 1960s , line 3 transports crude oil 1,097 miles from edmonton , alberta , across northern minnesota .
tribal and environmental groups opposed to the proposed enbridge line 3 project rally in september 2017 .
