james anaya , the un special rapporteur on the rights of indigenous peoples , has concluded that there is no way justice will ever be possible in the united states , as long as the government continues to hold illegally-seized native american land .
anaya said that he spent nearly two weeks of visiting indian reservations , communities and natives living in cities .
at the end of the investigation , he concluded that in all contexts , police brutality and systemic racism has been pervasive .
