26 aug 2018 , 09:30 peoples ' democratic congress-europe condemned in a written statement the attack ordered by the state against the saturday mothers in istanbul on 25 august .
for hours the saturday mothers and participants were subjected to violent attacks and repression , yet , as the statement stressed , they could carry out their action .
the hdk-a pointed out that in many european countries , as soon as the images of the police repression on galatasaray went online , people held demonstrations to support the saturday mothers .
